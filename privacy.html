Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Crew Voice ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website mycrewvoice.com. This page describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Industry
  • Number of crew members
  • Business description

If you subscribe to our email list, we collect your email address through our email provider, MailerLite.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects information such as how often you visit, what pages you view, and what other sites you visited before coming to ours. We use this data only to improve our website. Google Analytics collects the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit, but we do not combine this with any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and contact requests
  • Send you information about our services if you request it
  • Send email communications if you subscribe to our list
  • Improve our website and services

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website:

  • Web3Forms — processes contact form submissions
  • MailerLite — manages email subscriptions and delivers email communications
  • Google Analytics — provides website usage analytics
  • Netlify — hosts our website

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share your information with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate our website and respond to your inquiries.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our email list, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in every email we send.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies through Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
